About

Trivialand was created in late 2010, but it never really took off. The channel was shut down (but not dropped) for a couple of months. Then, in about Springtime of 2011, it was reopened. A new bot was placed, which used the popular trivia script Bogus Trivia. Tannn3r, the owner, asked some people he knew to come and try it out. It became popular very quickly and the channel was always busy for a couple of months.

Overtime, members have reported bad questions and sent in feedback to make the game better. We have over 80,000 questions circulating and an active member base full of competitive players who love to play.

So finally, here we are. You can read our Future Plans to see our plans for the future. Freenode isn’t a huge gaming network, but it’s nice to play a round of trivia from time to time. Enjoy the game!

-Tanner (aka tann)
