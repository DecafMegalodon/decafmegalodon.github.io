Cloaks

Cloak Perks:

  • Automatic channel voice
  • Flood kick rate limit raised
  • Hides your IP
  • Recognized as an established channel member
  • Bragging rights (everyone loves swag)
  • Promotes our channel
  • Prove to others how smart you are

Cloaks available:

The following cloaks are available to all players. As you gain more points, you unlock more cloaks to choose from. However, you can still choose lower level cloaks. Cloaks with an asterisk require you to be registered with Glime. All cloaks require being registered on Freenode. Points refer to Year Over Year points, meaning all the points you have earned in the past few years. You will need to calculate this on your own by using the Score Archive.

  • Over 6 million – choose your own category!*
  • trivialand/nick – over 4.5 million points required*
  • trivialand/elite/nick – over 4 million points required
  • trivialand/addicted/nick – over 3 million points required
  • trivialand/distinguished/nick – over 2.5 million points required
  • trivialand/genius/nick – over 2 million points required
  • trivialand/master/nick – over 1.5 million points required
  • trivialand/player/nick – over 1 million points required
  • trivialand/student/nick – over 500,000 points required
  • trivialand/guesser/nick – over 100,000 points required

Special Cloaks:

These cloaks are not available to all players. They have to be unlocked by different means, however, they contain the same perks as the other cloaks. All of these cloaks require registration with Glime.

  • trivialand/donor/nick – See this page.
  • trivialand/editor/nick – You have fixed over 150 questions.
  • trivialand/beta/nick – Been a player since the beginning.
  • trivialand/developer/nick – Contributed code to the TriviaTime project
  • Dual cloaks are available to players who have been around for at least three months.
