FAQ

New to the channel and have some questions? We have compiled a list of the most frequently asked questions to help you get around. New and updated questions will be specified for existing players who want to stay up-to-date.

I said an answer first, but someone who said it after me got the points.

Usually this happens because the lag between your client and the bot is greater than the lag between the bot and another person. It happens. Try to be a little quicker next time or connecting to the same server as Glime. You do not need to worry about extra spaces in your answer as they are ignored. Use .ping to get your ping reply.

Can I keep my score if I change my nickname?

Due to potential abuse, scores are tied to nicknames only. Any nickname changes will NOT be merged to the new nickname, but instead treated as a new player. After speaking with the owner of the script, a new workaround is currently in the works. Read more.

Who do I contact when there is a problem in the channel?

Join #trivialand-ops and ping all of the voiced users. If you do not get a reply within a few minutes, send a memo to tann. For more information about sending memos, see here.

What is a KAOS question?

A KAOS question required more than one answer for the question. For example, if the question was “Popular IRC Networks, Ten Possible Answers”, you would need to answer with ten IRC networks (each in a new line) to complete the question.

How do I get a bonus for KAOS questions?

After all of the answers have been said, a bonus will be given to everyone who answered. If nobody gets a bonus, the question had less than ten answers and wasn’t eligible for a bonus. If you were the only one who gave the answers, no bonus will be given.

How do I get a cloak?

Visit this page to see if you qualify for a cloak and follow the directions to get one there.

How many questions are there?

Our channel has the most questions out of any trivia channel on Freenode! To get the most accurate and up-to-date statistics, use the .info command in the channel.

I am new, how can I catch up to other people?

Enjoy the game and be quick. Participate in KAOS to get the bonus, and make your goal to get auto-voiced by being a Top Player. One baby step at a time. When the score resets every day, week, month, and year, try to make it in the top ten for each of those stats. In no time, you’ll be caught up. Being more active in the channel can also help you achieve a higher ranking.

How can I merge my score between multiple nicknames?

Simply fill out the form below and your scores will be merged soon. You may need to submit this form more than once if you’ve had multiple nicknames.

I have a question about registering.

Please read the register page first. If you still have questions, leave a comment on that page or contact an op.

I don’t want the game to start when I join the channel. Can you take that off?

You will automatically be exempt from the auto-start list when you register with Glime. However, you must be identified or using the account hostmask, otherwise you will not be recognized and the game may still start. See this post for more information.

How do I report questions, and what is it for?

Please consider fixing the question yourself instead of reporting it. When reporting, specify the question number and a brief summary of the error so it can be fixed in the future. You can view current reports here.

I was kicked for flooding, but I was just trying to answer a question.

Sorry about that. Unfortunately, we have to keep flood protection in the channel. People have tried to flood it many times before. You can rejoin at anytime. You can register to have the limit raised.

Why can’t I stop the trivia game?

Trivia doesn’t need to be stopped as it will stop automatically when people stop playing. Still, if you must be able to stop the game, you will earn that right when you register. Use your new powers wisely.
