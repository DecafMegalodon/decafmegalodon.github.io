Stepping Down

Posted on October 20, 2013 by tann
1

Trivialand has overcome my honest expectations. When it started we had no regulars for quite a while. Then, suddenly we had over 100 people playing and an average of 20 people in the channel at once. Today, over 500 people have played the game and the channel averages at 50 people in the channel.

This was all new to me. At the beginning I had one goal: to host a trivia game for all to enjoy. That goal soon became to host the best trivia game across IRC. Surprisingly, Trivialand seems to have won out the competition. Other networks are losing users fast and Trivialand is the most popular trivia channel on Freenode.

How did this happen? A lot of hard work and determination. Through free hosts and servers, I have managed to keep my cost relatively low. My time into the channel, however, was instead at an all time high. I spent countless hours every week into the game and the channel. Apparently, it was worth it.

In the beginning I was very passionate about the channel. I loved doing it, for the game and the community. Very quickly, however, it became more of a chore and the tasks continued to pile up. I was running the show alone with some support from other players. Although I appreciated their help, I knew this couldn’t last forever.

Things are better now, but instead I never play anymore. I’m on IRC maybe a few minutes every day, if I’m lucky. I have been slowly deviating away from IRC over the years, but Trivialand has kept me there. But now, it is time to move on.

I am grateful for the community and all the amazing people I have come across. Many lessons have been learned and I still believe Trivialand was a success. If I could start over many things would be different, but I am still thankful for how it all turned out.

So what happens next? TriviaTime, Trivialand’s exclusive trivia plugin, will be a priority for me over Trivialand. I do still plan to create the plugin. As for the channel itself, I’d rather many things to unchanged. Still, I would like to appoint a new chief operator for the channel. This person will be responsible for hosting the bot, website, domain, server, and maintains the channel. If you are interested and would like more details, please email me at tann@trivialand.org as soon as possible.

To current, future, and past players, I would like to thank you for playing and any contributions you have made. I want you to know Trivialand is not dying. All that is occurring is a simple transfer of ownership.

I will see some of you very soon for the development of TriviaTime.

Announcing TrivaTime: #trivialand’s own trivia plugin

Posted on June 17, 2013 by tann
Reply

I have an exciting announcement to make. It has been a long dream of mine to make my own trivia plugin. However, I do not know any programming languages. Still, I am determined to make a better trivia plugin that meets all of the channel’s needs. Now, while I am finishing up learning Python, I am announcing our new project: TriviaTime.

Glime uses runs on an old version of eggdrop that then uses an outdated trivia script (BogusTrivia). Eggdrop scripts are made in TCL, and I was not interested in learning TCL as it is not widely used. However, SupyMonkey runs on Supybot, which uses Python. Python is a language that is used for a variety of tasks, and I determined it would be a great fit.

TriviaTime will be a plugin, written in Python, that we will use to replace our current trivia script, Bogus Trivia on Glime. We want to start this project by 2014 and finish by 2015. We are planning on keeping many things unchanged, while improving everything else. New features will not be introduced until after the project is finished.

If you are interested in assisting us in the development of this plugin, please view this page. For more information about the design of TriviaTime, click here. We are very excited to get started and can’t wait to bring this new plugin to the channel in 2015. Still, we have a lot of work to do and could use all the help we can get. Don’t know any programming languages? You can still help.
