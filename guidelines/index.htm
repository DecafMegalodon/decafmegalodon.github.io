Guidelines

Freenode Policy:

Be an effective catalyst. This will get you far here, the Internet, and life.

Prevent the discussion from becoming off-topic. You may be reported to Freenode staff for violating this policy.

Channel Guidelines:

Unauthorized robots and scripts are strictly forbidden. This includes, but is not limited to, shortcut commands that allow you to type less of an answer.

Answers should not be obtained by any third party. This includes, but is not limited to, search engines, logs, and saved answers. Each answer must come from your brain and your brain alone.

Sexism, racism, hateful speech and harassment of any kind is not tolerated. Be courteous to other players. Do not troll around.

Keep the channel family friendly. Users of all ages are allowed to play here, and weâ€™d like to keep it that way. This includes avoiding sensitive topics or distasteful jokes.

If you must use swear words, use them sparingly. It may be time to broaden your vocabulary.

Commands and features for any and all of the channel bots are not to be abused. Do not flood the channel.

Report any and all issues to operators in #trivialand-ops. If no operator is available, refer to the topic.

Cloak Guidelines:

Cloak wearers are expected to abide by all Freenode networks guidelines and channel guidelines, plus our channel guidelines above.

Cloaks can be denied and revoked at anytime, with or without notice.

Cloaks are not to be updated within the first fourteen days after a cloak is applied.

Failure to follow Freenode policy, Channel or Cloak Guidelines may result in being banned from the channel.

These guidelines were last updated July 28th, 2014.
