Register

Why register?

Registering with Glime is optional, but it will secure your points to you. This is the ultimate, fool-proof way to protecting your points. Your password is encrypted and stored on a secure server, although it is still recommended you use a strong password that is not used for any other account.

Note: This is not for registering an account on Freenode. See these instructions to register on the Freenode IRC network.

Quick Registration

Registering is so simple, easy, and fast, that even your grandma could do it in under two minutes. Just message Glime starting with the word register, followed by your username and a strong, never-used-before password. After that, you can (optionally) add your hostmask to your account so you can identify with Glime automatically (and be identified even when you change your username). Need to identify to Glime?

Message Glime identify, followed by your account name and password. Like this:

/msg Glime identify baduser badpassword
