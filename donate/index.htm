Donate

Donating to Trivialand is a great way to make the experience better for everyone involved. I get asked a lot by players as to how they can help out, but they don’t even realize they already are! Here are some ways you can help us out, too.

  • Report questions. This is the best way to contribute. By reporting questions, you create a better experience for the rest of the players.
  • Report problems. Sometimes problems come up. Glime screws up, the channel is being attacked by aliens, or somebody is cheating. Reporting these issues makes you the channel hero for the day.
  • Add questions. You will enhance the quality and quantity of our questions. Different and new questions create a unique and enjoyable channel atmosphere. New questions and KAOS can be submitted at anytime. Interested? Ask for more details.
  • Fix reported questions. Hundreds of questions have been reported and can cause a lot of strain on staff to fix them in a timely manner. Help us out and you’ll be doing everyone a favor! Click here for more details.
  • Be involved. Sometimes we’ll host polls, surveys, and other fun stuff for the community. By getting involved, you contribute to an entertaining environment.
  • Make a monetary contribution. Not everything is free, and Trivialand is no exception. Hosting, domain, and server fees can really add up and be costly. You can help. Learn more.
